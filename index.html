About us

With a clear goal and a common set of values, these pioneers began to build the foundation of the NGO "GUARDIANS OF THE TAILS". Their first mission was to identify and address the urgent needs of the homeless animal community. Through fundraising, responsible adoptions and awareness campaigns, the NGO has been able to provide a safe and loving home for hundreds of abandoned animals.
